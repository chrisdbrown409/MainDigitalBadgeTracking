Digital Badge Tracking Platforms

Digital badges have somewhat taken off in the last decade. Digitial badges can be earned by taking courses or passing tests. Badges can be saved to many locations like a LinkedIn profile or added to an email signature line. Also, many companies prefer candidates that have certifications. Therefore, tracking badges have become just as important as earning them. Therefore, applicaitons are being built for that purpose.
